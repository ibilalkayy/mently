FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mently?

Mently is a personalized learning platform that connects you directly with real mentor. Instead of taking generic, pre-recorded courses, you get live guidance, ongoing updates, and personal advice — so you can grow faster.

Is this a course platform?

No. Mently is not about courses — it's about real-time, 1:1 mentorship depending on your needs.

How do I get matched with a mentor?

You can easily get matched with a mentor from the "Meet Our Amazing Mentors" section, look at their services and choose a mentor and his service(s) to continue.

How does the pricing work?

Each service price is dependent on the mentor. We just take a small commission from that money but before paying us, you can checkout the mentor profile and intro section. After that, you can request a particular service.

Will I get the money-back guarantee?

Yes, we'll give you the money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied after first 20% result and we won't ask any question.

How I would be communicating with a mentor in real-time?

All the communication will happen in the discord server where we'll add you and the mentor in a private category and you can communicate and ask questions there.
